Encapsule

To create a website for EC Department, in accordance with the CWC (Cyber Woven Campus), as stated in the Resurgence Manifesto. This website will provide project notifications, subject-wise notes and other educational notifications that will be aiding the students' academics.

Cultural Community

A cultural community will be formed in our department, that will bring together students of every batch along with their creative and artistic abilities. This will bring together students of all years, making a bond that lasts.

Placement Assistance

There will be core and non-core placement training provided for the students of EC department. This will help them land job offers from their dream companies. These trainings include, coding practices, mock aptitude and placement tests and other helpful materials.

Edam

Edam is a place where students can hangout and interact with each other, spend leisure time and share thoughts. This can be a place where students can share many memorable moments together.

Linkedin Community

EC department has a great history of many successful alumni. To utilise this, and to make a close knitted community for helping all students of ECE department in their choice of careers, Linkedin is the best platform. This professional social media platform, will not only help you in reaching out to these alumnis, but also will give you a wider reach and increase your chances in getting internships and job offers.

GoGeek

GoGeek Component Store is of great necessity in the EC department. Therefore, this store will be revived, along with an app which will provide the options for pre-ordering, viewing the component list and other features.

Enlight

"Enlight" - technical and cultural fest will be held to showcase both the technical skills and cultural and artistic skills of the students of EC department. This will also include Electronight - a fun and vibrant night just for the EC department and Electroleague - an intradepartment football league for EC department.

Department Magazine

A magazine to encourage and exhibit the multi faceted talents and to boost creativity of the students of EC department. This magazine will have a silver lining, which will remain in our hearts forever.
